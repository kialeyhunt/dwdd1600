ABOUT THE AUTHOR

K.A. Hunt

Kialey Hunt was born with a lust for the unknown and the out-of-reach. She's been daydreaming and fantasizing for as long as she can remember, always yearning for stories that are beyond our realm and take her elsewhere. It is of no surprise to anyone who knows her that her first novel would be about dragons -- and if she could be one, she would.

Kialey enjoys spending time outside, walking through forests and fields, birding, attempting snapshots of animals with her phone, and dancing in the rain. She hopes to get a cat and/or a dog (or more) very soon. She spends most of her days writing and taking care of her daughter, and she and her husband are in love with the Scottish Highlands and plan to move there soon.
